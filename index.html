Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Chat2PDF is a client-side web application that values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

Chat2PDF operates entirely in your browser and does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

3. Local Storage

The application uses browser localStorage to save:

  • Your font size preferences

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

4. Content Processing

All content you enter, edit, or preview in Chat2PDF:

  • Is processed entirely in your browser
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Remains completely private and under your control

5. Third-Party Services

Chat2PDF loads the following libraries from CDNs for functionality:

  • Marked.js (Markdown parsing)
  • KaTeX (mathematical formula rendering)

These libraries are loaded for technical functionality only and do not collect your data.

6. Cookies

This application does not use cookies.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can clear localStorage at any time through your browser settings
  • You can delete your content by clearing the editor or closing the browser

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our GitHub repository.
